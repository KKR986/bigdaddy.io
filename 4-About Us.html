Our History

Big Daddy Learning Center is a private school offering day care center respectively.
It is owned and operated by Partner and business sponsored by Northridge Academy.
The school was founded in May 2023 upon the encouragement and recommendation
and approval of the Deped Baguio. On its few months of operation the school was
granted by the Department of Education, Culture and Sports (DECS)
the permit No. EE 06 s 2023. At that time,
there were 07 student enrollees handled by 4 teachers .

In May 2023, the school was able to comply with all the requirements for
recognition thus, was granted Government
Recognition No. PS-035 by the DECS. In 2023 the school applied
for the opening of Day Care Class. This was given permission in 2023
through Government Permit (R-1) No. EED-001 s 2023. The year 2023
ushered in the opening of its complete elementary course under
Government Permit No. EED 0010 s. 2023.

From its humble beginnings, the school continues to grow and strive
for excellence in its academic and co-curricular programs. Through the
abled leadership of School Teachers, Big Daddy Learning Center
continually exerts all efforts to be of service to the learning community
striving to provide updated, relevant and holistic education
for the students under its care.
